Ali ADALI, Ph. D.
Mathematician, Educator, and Problem Solver

Welcome! I am a passionate Turkish mathematician with a journey rooted in the thrill of mathematical competitions, where I earned two bronze medals at the International Mathematical Olympiad. My academic foundation was built at Bilkent University in Türkiye, where I completed both my undergraduate studies in mathematics and a master's program focusing on number theory. My doctoral studies at The Ohio State University then led me to explore the field of fluid mechanics.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of sharing my knowledge and enthusiasm for mathematics. This includes instructing at national math olympiad camps in Türkiye, serving as a postdoctoral fellow and lecturer at The Ohio State University, holding a position as an assistant professor at Yeditepe University, and guiding students as a math olympiad instructor at AlphaStar Academy in California.

Currently, as an independent researcher, instructor, and olympiad coach, this website serves as a platform to introduce myself, showcase my portfolio, and connect with potential collaborators, employers, or clients. I am actively developing engaging mathematical content, including short courses, proofs, and problem solutions, and I am available for olympiad coaching and instructorship opportunities.
